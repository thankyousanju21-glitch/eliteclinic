The Elite Clinic Story

Founded to bridge the gap between high-end corporate hospitals and local primary care centers, Elite Medical and General Store & Elite Multi Speciality Clinic was established in Hadosiddhapura, Carmelaram. We recognized the need for a unified healthcare hub where patients could consult top doctors and immediately fulfill their medical prescriptions without waiting.

Our Mission

To deliver evidence-based, compassionate primary care while ensuring absolute convenience through our integrated pharmacy and general store, reducing the friction in healthcare access.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and sought-after neighborhood healthcare destination in the Carmelaram and Sarjapur micro-market, synonymous with quality, affordability, and zero wait times.
