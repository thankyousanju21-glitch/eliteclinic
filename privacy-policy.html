Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data security are our top priorities.

Last Updated:

At Elite Medical and General Store & Elite Multi Speciality Clinic, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our patients and website visitors. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Data Collection

We collect personal and medical information only when voluntarily provided by you. This may occur when you:

  • Book an appointment via our website or WhatsApp.
  • Fill out patient registration forms at the clinic.
  • Contact us via phone, email, or social media.

The information we collect may include your name, age, gender, contact details, and medical history.

2. Appointment Information & WhatsApp Usage

Our website utilizes a WhatsApp redirect feature for appointment booking. Please note:

  • When you use the "Book via WhatsApp" button, the data entered in the form is converted into a pre-filled text message.
  • This message is sent securely through your personal WhatsApp account directly to our clinic's official WhatsApp number.
  • We do not store this form data in any backend website database.

3. Use of Information

The information collected is used strictly for:

  • Scheduling and managing your medical appointments.
  • Providing appropriate medical diagnosis, treatment, and pharmacy services.
  • Communicating essential updates, follow-ups, and test results.
  • Improving our clinic's services and user experience.

4. Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use basic cookies to enhance user experience and analyze website traffic. These cookies do not collect personally identifiable medical information. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though some website features may not function optimally.

5. Third Party Services

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. However, we may share information with trusted third parties (such as diagnostic labs) only when necessary for your medical treatment and with your implicit or explicit consent.

6. Your User Rights

As a patient, you have the right to:

  • Request access to your medical records held by the clinic.
  • Request corrections to any inaccurate personal information.
  • Withdraw consent for future data processing (subject to legal and medical record retention requirements).
